Before submitting a report: PM & AQI meaning

Shared Terms

PM1.0 / PM2.5 / PM10

These are particle size groups measured in µg/m³. Lower size means deeper lung penetration and often faster indoor spread.

How AQI is used

AQI summarizes PM2.5 into a readable risk band so teams can decide if immediate action is needed.

Helpful report detail

Include time, location, and whether PM stayed high or cleared quickly after ventilation.
