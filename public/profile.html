Staff Knowledge

PM1.0

Tracks very fine aerosol that travels fast indoors and can indicate fresh vaping activity.

PM2.5 + AQI

PM2.5 is converted to AQI to show health impact quickly. Higher AQI means higher urgency.

PM10

Adds larger-particle context for facility conditions, cleaning load, and ventilation checks.
